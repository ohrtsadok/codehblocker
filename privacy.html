Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025

This Chrome extension is designed for use on school-managed Chrome devices to control direct navigation to specific educational websites.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, browsing history, or user activity.

How the Extension Works

All functionality is performed locally on the device. The extension checks whether a navigation matches a specific domain and, if so, redirects the user to a local block page. No page content is read or analyzed.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use analytics, tracking tools, advertising services, or third-party APIs. No information is sent to external servers.

Offline Use

The block page is provided directly by the extension and continues to work even when the device is offline.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact the school or organization responsible for managing this extension.
