Privacy Policy This Chrome extension is designed for use on school-managed devices. Data Collection This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, browsing history, or user activity. Data Usage All functionality is performed locally on the device. The extension only checks whether a navigation matches a specific domain in order to redirect the user to a local block page. Third Parties No data is sent to any third party. No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used. Contact If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact the school or organization managing this extension.
